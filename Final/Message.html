A Message From Branch Counselor It is a matter of pride to pen down a message for the students who are willing to join the student branch of VGEC IEEE. IEEE is a pavement of learning technical abilities and making a professional profile about your accomplishments. You will get the opportunity to meet and learn from the fellow students, faculty member, Brainy scholars and professionals in the field. You will get a chance to be part of workshops, hands-on sessions and webinar in order to acquaint the students with recent trends and development in technology. IEEE encourages the environment of experimentation and novelty for a batter cause. IEEE is a platform for students to express the technical pursuit which develops in their originality of thought and perception.
